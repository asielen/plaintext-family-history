THE MILLBROOK GAZETTE - March 16, 2008
OBITUARIES

Robert "Bob" Rivera, 77, of Millbrook, died Friday, March 14, 2008.
Born in the Bronx in 1930, he served in the U.S. Army in Korea (1951-1953) before
settling in Millbrook, where he ran Rivera's Grocery on Main Street for thirty-five
years. He is survived by his wife, Maria, and their children Michael and Carol.
