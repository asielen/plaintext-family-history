## Notes
My grandfather - everyone called him "Bob." He grew up in the Bronx, served in the
Army in Korea from 1951 to 1953 [[Robert Rivera obituary 2008]], and afterward ran
a small grocery on Main Street in Millbrook for thirty-five years. He met Maria in
Dallas after the war and they came east together.
