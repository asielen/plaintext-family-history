# Carol Rivera

**Born:** 30 October 1954 - Millbrook, New York
**Parents:** [[Robert Rivera]] and [[Maria Garcia]]
**Siblings:** [[Michael Rivera]]

## Notes
My dad's older sister - "Aunt Carol." Not a direct ancestor, so she lives in
`connections/` rather than a numbered folder. She never married, taught art at the
Millbrook high school, and kept every photo and document the family ever produced -
she's the reason half this archive exists. Still sharp as a tack and the best person
to interview about the Rivera side.
