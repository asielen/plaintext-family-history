What happens when, and who does it

Landing
Explorer drag, the drop zone above, or fha capture --ingest - all identical: bytes in inbox/, nothing else.
Filing (a click)
File as a source runs Stage A of fha process: mints the S-id, moves and renames the document into documents/, scaffolds the source record. Deterministic, dry-run-gated - a button can own it.
Reading the evidence (the AI lane)
Drafting claims from the document - reading the scan, extracting names and dates - is judgment work. It stays with Claude (process-source) or with you (Add a claim on the source page). Serve never guesses at your evidence.
Review
Anything drafted lands as suggested and waits on the Review page, grouped under its source.
