# Connections (the "FAN club")

People who are part of your family's story but aren't your direct ancestors:
aunts and uncles, cousins, in-laws, close friends, neighbors, business partners.
Genealogists call them the **F**riends, **A**ssociates, and **N**eighbors - the
people *around* an ancestor. Researching them is often how a brick wall finally
falls.

Drop a person file in here the same way you'd fill in an ancestor: copy the shape
of one of the numbered person files. No special format needed.
